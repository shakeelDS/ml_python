Data Dictionary

Listings
--------
Contains airbnb property information from 5 English cities scraped.
Obtained from:
http://insideairbnb.com/get-the-data.html

Neighbourhoods
-------------
Contains mappings of neighbourhood names used in listings to wider neighbourhood groups if they exist. The neighbourhoods correspond to either district (such as in London, boroughs), or wards (such as areas of a city in Manchester).
